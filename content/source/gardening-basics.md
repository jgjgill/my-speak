# 1단계: 한글 스크립트

요즘 정원에 관심이 많아요. **어디서부터 시작해야 할까요?**{Where should I start?}
**초보자에게는 키우기 쉬운 허브나 다육식물부터 시작하는 것이 좋아요.**{It's good to start with easy-to-grow herbs or succulents for beginners.}
**물은 얼마나 자주 줘야 하나요?**{How often should I water them?}
식물마다 다르지만, 흙이 말랐을 때 주는 것이 일반적이에요. **겉흙이 말랐는지 확인해보세요.**{Check if the topsoil is dry.}
**햇빛은 얼마나 필요한가요?**{How much sunlight do they need?}
대부분의 허브는 하루에 6시간 정도의 햇빛이 필요해요. **밝은 간접광이 좋아요.**{Bright indirect light is better than direct sunlight.}
알겠습니다. **오늘 바로 작은 화분부터 사러 가야겠어요.**{I should go buy a small pot today.}

# 2단계: 외국어 스크립트

Hello, I'm very interested in gardening these days. Where should I start?
It's good to start with easy-to-grow herbs or succulents for beginners.
How often should I water them?
It varies by plant, but it's common to water when the soil is dry. Check if the topsoil is dry.
How much sunlight do they need?
Most herbs need about 6 hours of sunlight per day. Bright indirect light is better than direct sunlight.
I understand. I should go buy a small pot today.
