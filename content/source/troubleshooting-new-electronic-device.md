# 1단계: 한글 스크립트

안녕하세요, 새로 산 이어폰이 작동을 안 해요.
이런, **어떤 문제가 있나요**{What seems to be the problem?}?
전원이 켜지지 않아요. **충전은 제대로 했나요**{Did you charge it properly?}?
네, 밤새 충전했어요. **다른 문제는 없었나요**{Was there anything else}?
아니요, 처음부터 안 됐어요. **이것 좀 봐주시겠어요**{Could you take a look at this}?
알겠습니다. **연결을 확인해 볼게요**{Let me check the connection}.

# 2단계: 외국어 스크립트

Hello, my new earphones aren't working.
Oh no, what seems to be the problem?
It won't turn on. Did you charge it properly?
Yes, I charged it all night. Was there anything else?
No, it didn't work from the start. Could you take a look at this?
Okay. Let me check the connection.
