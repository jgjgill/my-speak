# 1단계: 한글 스크립트

안녕하세요, **대출 상담을 받고 싶습니다**{I would like to get a loan consultation}.
네, **어떤 종류의 대출**{what kind of loan}을 생각하고 계신가요?
**주택 담보 대출**{home mortgage loan}에 대해 알고 싶습니다.
알겠습니다. **소득 증빙 서류**{income verification documents}를 **준비해 오셨나요**{Have you prepared}?
네, 여기 있습니다.
감사합니다. 그럼 함께 **서류를 검토해 보겠습니다**{Let's review the documents}.

# 2단계: 외국어 스크립트

Hello, I would like to get a loan consultation.
Yes, what kind of loan are you considering?
I would like to know about a home mortgage loan.
Understood. Have you prepared income verification documents?
Yes, here they are.
Thank you. Let's review the documents together.
