# 1단계: 한글 스크립트

안녕하세요, **무엇을 찾으세요**{what are you looking for}?
이 셔츠 **얼마예요**{how much is this}?
**입어봐도 될까요**{Can I try this on}?
음... 사이즈가 **너무 작아요**{It's too small}.
혹시 **더 큰 사이즈**{a larger size} 있나요?
네, **이걸로 할게요**{I'll take this one}.
감사합니다.

# 2단계: 외국어 스크립트

Hello, what are you looking for?
How much is this shirt?
Can I try this on?
Hmm... It's too small.
Do you have a larger size?
Yes, I'll take this one.
Thank you.
