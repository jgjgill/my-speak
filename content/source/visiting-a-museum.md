# 1단계: 한글 스크립트

오늘 시간 괜찮으면 **같이 박물관 갈래**{Do you want to go to the museum together}?
**좋은 생각이야**{That's a great idea}! **무엇을 볼 수 있을까**{What can we see}?
**새로운 특별 전시**{A new special exhibition}가 열리고 있대. **표는 미리 예매하는 게 좋겠어**{It would be good to book tickets in advance}.
**그래, 알겠어**{Okay, got it}. **몇 시에 문 열어**{What time does it open}?
**오전 10시부터**{From 10 AM} 오후 6시까지야. **그럼 2시에 만날까**{Shall we meet at 2 PM then}?
**완벽해**{Perfect}!

# 2단계: 외국어 스크립트

Do you want to go to the museum together?
That's a great idea! What can we see?
They say there's a new special exhibition. It would be good to book tickets in advance.
Okay, got it. What time does it open?
From 10 AM to 6 PM. Shall we meet at 2 PM then?
Perfect!

# 4단계: 키워드 스피치

## 레벨 1: 상세한 키워드 (70% 정보)

박물관 갈래 → Do you want to go to the museum together?
좋은 생각이야, 무엇을 볼 수 있을까 → That's a great idea! What can we see?
특별 전시, 표, 미리 예매 → A new special exhibition. It would be good to book tickets in advance.
몇 시에 문 열어 → Okay, got it. What time does it open?
오전 10시, 2시에 만날까 → From 10 AM to 6 PM. Shall we meet at 2 PM then?
완벽해 → Perfect!
