# 1단계: 한글 스크립트

안녕하세요, 제 고양이 레오 **정기 검진을 예약했는데요**{I have an appointment for a regular check-up}.
네, 확인되었습니다. 잠시만 기다려주세요.
최근에 레오가 어디 불편한 곳은 없었나요?
아니요, 특별한 문제는 없었어요.
**식욕은 어때요**{How is his appetite}? 평소처럼 잘 먹나요?
네, 밥도 잘 먹고 간식도 좋아해요.
알겠습니다. 오늘 예방접종도 같이 할 수 있을까요?
네, 수의사 선생님과 상담 후에 가능합니다.
혹시 **특별히 걱정되는 부분**{any particular concerns}이 있으신가요?
음, 가끔 **재채기를 하는 것 같아요**{seems to sneeze sometimes}.
알겠습니다. 그 부분도 진료 때 말씀드릴게요.
이제 진료실로 들어가시면 됩니다.

# 2단계: 외국어 스크립트

Hello, I have an appointment for a regular check-up for my cat, Leo.
Okay, I've confirmed it. Please wait a moment.
Has Leo shown any signs of discomfort recently?
No, there haven't been any particular problems.
How is his appetite? Is he eating well as usual?
Yes, he eats his meals well and loves his treats.
I see. Yes, can we also get his vaccinations today?
Yes, that's possible after consulting with the veterinarian.
Do you have any particular concerns?
Well, he seems to sneeze sometimes.
Okay. I'll mention that during the examination.
You can go into the exam room now.

# 4단계: 키워드 스피치

## 레벨 1: 상세한 키워드 (70% 정보)

고양이 정기 검진, 예약했어요 → Hello, I have an appointment for a regular check-up for my cat, Leo.
최근 불편한 점, 있었나요 → Has Leo shown any signs of discomfort recently?
오늘 예방접종, 가능할까요 → Can we also get his vaccinations today?
가끔 재채기, 하는 것 같아요 → He seems to sneeze sometimes.

## 레벨 2: 핵심 키워드 (50% 정보)

정기 검진 예약 → I have an appointment for a regular check-up.
식욕은 어떤가요 → How is his appetite?
예방접종 가능 여부 → Can we also get his vaccinations today?
걱정되는 부분 → Do you have any particular concerns?

## 레벨 3: 최소한의 키워드 (30% 정보)

예약 → I have an appointment.
식욕 → How is his appetite?
예방접종 → Can we get his vaccinations?
걱정 → Any concerns?

## 레벨 4: 외국어 키워드

Appointment, check-up → I have an appointment for a regular check-up.
Appetite → How is his appetite?
Vaccinations → Can we also get his vaccinations today?
Concerns, sneeze → Do you have any particular concerns? He seems to sneeze sometimes.
