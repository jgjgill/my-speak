# 1단계: 한글 스크립트

아, 이 **버스 또 늦네**{this bus is late again}.
응, **항상 그래**{it's always like this}. **오늘 사람도 너무 많아**{there are too many people today too}.
**이것 때문에 회사에 늦겠어**{I'm going to be late for work because of this}.
나도. **에어컨도 잘 안 나오는 것 같아**{the air conditioning doesn't seem to be working well either}. **너무 더워**{it's so hot}.
**지하철 탈 걸 그랬어**{I should have taken the subway}.
**다음엔 지하철 타자**{let's take the subway next time}.

# 2단계: 외국어 스크립트

Oh, this bus is late again.
Yeah, it's always like this. There are too many people today too.
I'm going to be late for work because of this.
Me too. The air conditioning doesn't seem to be working well either. It's so hot.
I should have taken the subway.
Let's take the subway next time.
