# 1단계: 한글 스크립트

안녕하세요, 악기 배우는 것에 대해 궁금한 점이 있어요.
네, 어떤 점이 궁금하신가요?
저는 **기타를 배우고 싶은데, 처음 시작하기에 좋은 악기일까요?**{I want to learn the guitar, is it a good instrument to start with?}
네, 기타는 초보자에게 인기가 많아요. **운반이 쉽고**{easy to carry} **다양한 장르의 음악을 연주할 수 있다**{can play various genres of music}는 장점이 있습니다.
그렇군요. **그럼 시작하려면 어떤 준비가 필요한가요?**{Then, what preparations are needed to start?}
**기본적인 기타와**{a basic guitar and} **튜너**{a tuner} 정도면 충분합니다. **온라인 강좌**{online courses}나 **레슨**{lessons}을 병행하면 더 빨리 배울 수 있어요.
감사합니다! **어서 빨리 기타를 연주해보고 싶어요.**{I can't wait to play the guitar.}

# 2단계: 외국어 스크립트

Hello, I have some questions about learning a musical instrument.
Yes, what are you curious about?
I want to learn the guitar, is it a good instrument to start with?
Yes, the guitar is very popular among beginners. It's **easy to carry** and you **can play various genres of music**, which are its advantages.
I see. **Then, what preparations are needed to start?**
A **basic guitar and** a **tuner** are enough. You can learn faster by taking **online courses** or **lessons** concurrently.
Thank you! **I can't wait to play the guitar.**
