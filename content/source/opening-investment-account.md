# 1단계: 한글 스크립트

안녕하세요, 투자 계좌 개설을 문의하려고 합니다.
네, 처음이신가요? **어떤 종류의 투자 계좌**{What kind of investment account}에 관심 있으신가요?
주식이나 펀드에 투자하고 싶은데, **처음 시작하기 좋은 계좌**{a good account to start with}가 있을까요?
네, **가장 기본적인 투자 계좌**{the most basic investment account}가 있습니다. **필요한 서류**{What documents are needed}가 무엇인가요?
신분증과 **최근 소득 증명서**{recent proof of income}가 필요합니다.
서류 작성 후 제출하시면, **보통 며칠 내로 계좌가 개설**{the account is usually opened within a few days}됩니다.
알겠습니다, **안내 감사합니다**{Thank you for the guidance}.

# 2단계: 외국어 스크립트

Hello, I would like to inquire about opening an investment account.
Yes, is this your first time? What kind of investment account are you interested in?
I want to invest in stocks or funds, is there a good account to start with?
Yes, we have the most basic investment account. What documents are needed?
You will need your ID card and recent proof of income.
After you fill out the forms and submit them, the account is usually opened within a few days.
Okay, Thank you for the guidance.
