# 1단계: 한글 스크립트

이번 주말에 날씨가 아주 좋대.
우리 공원으로 **소풍 가는 거 어때**{How about going on a picnic}?
정말 **좋은 생각이야**{That's a great idea}!
내가 샌드위치랑 과일을 좀 **챙길게**{I'll pack}.
그럼 나는 마실 거랑 돗자리를 가져갈게.
우리 **언제 만날까**{When should we meet}?
토요일 오전 11시쯤 공원 입구에서 보자.

# 2단계: 외국어 스크립트

The weather is supposed to be great this weekend.
How about going on a picnic to the park?
That's a great idea!
I'll pack some sandwiches and fruit.
Then I'll bring drinks and a mat.
When should we meet?
Let's meet at the park entrance around 11 AM on Saturday.

# 2단계: 끊어읽기 버전

# 3단계: 사용자 자유 연습

1 ~ 2단계에서 구성한 콘텐츠 기반으로 한→외국어 스피킹 연습 진행

# 4단계: 키워드 스피치

## 레벨 1: 상세한 키워드 (70% 정보)

이번 주말, 날씨 좋대 → The weather is supposed to be great this weekend.
공원, 소풍 가는 거 어때 → How about going on a picnic to the park?
샌드위치, 과일, 챙길게 → I'll pack some sandwiches and fruit.
토요일 오전 11시, 공원 입구, 만나자 → Let's meet at the park entrance around 11 AM on Saturday.

## 레벨 2: 핵심 키워드 (50% 정보)

주말 날씨 → The weather is supposed to be great this weekend.
공원 소풍 제안 → How about going on a picnic to the park?
내가 챙길 것 → I'll pack some sandwiches and fruit.
만날 시간, 장소 → Let's meet at the park entrance around 11 AM on Saturday.

## 레벨 3: 최소한의 키워드 (30% 정보)

날씨 → The weather is supposed to be great this weekend.
소풍? → How about going on a picnic to the park?
음식 → I'll pack some sandwiches and fruit.
약속 → Let's meet at the park entrance around 11 AM on Saturday.

## 레벨 4: 외국어 키워드

Weather, weekend → The weather is supposed to be great this weekend.
Picnic, park → How about going on a picnic to the park?
Pack, sandwiches → I'll pack some sandwiches and fruit.
Meet, when, where → Let's meet at the park entrance around 11 AM on Saturday.
